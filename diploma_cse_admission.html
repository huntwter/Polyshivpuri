CSE Admissions


Admissions to the three-year Diploma program in Computer Science & Engineering (CSE) in Madhya Pradesh are offered to students who have passed Class 10th with First Division from a recognized board. The admission process is conducted by the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE), Madhya Pradesh through an online counseling system. No entrance exam is required. Admission is purely based on Class 10th merit, and students are allotted seats in various government and private polytechnic colleges across MP. The official admission notification is generally released between May and June, and counseling takes place in June-July through the official DTE MP website.

Details about cutoffs are available at Directorate Of technical education. For any queries or details related to admissions, e-mail us.
