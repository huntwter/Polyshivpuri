Academic Programs

At Government Polytechnic College, Shivpuri, we offer a variety of diploma programs designed to provide students with both theoretical knowledge and practical experience to excel in the rapidly evolving technical world. Our programs are industry-focused, preparing students for successful careers in engineering and technology.

Our Offered Programs:

  • Computer Science and Engineering - A program that focuses on computer systems, software development, and IT infrastructure.
  • Mechanical Engineering - A comprehensive program covering the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems.
  • Electrical Engineering - Focuses on electrical systems, circuits, and power generation.
  • Electronics Engineering - Covers the design and development of electronic devices and systems.

Each program is designed to foster a strong understanding of fundamental engineering principles, while also encouraging practical learning through internships, workshops, and industry collaborations. Our aim is to prepare students for both challenges and opportunities in the professional world.
