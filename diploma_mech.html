Diploma - Mechanical Engineering



The Diploma in Mechanical Engineering provides students with the necessary knowledge and skills in the design, development, and maintenance of mechanical systems. The program covers fundamental concepts such as mechanics, thermodynamics, fluid dynamics, materials science, and manufacturing processes. Students also gain hands-on experience through practical labs, projects, and industrial visits, ensuring they are well-prepared for a career in mechanical engineering or further academic studies.
