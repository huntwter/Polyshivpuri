Principal - Government Polytechnic College, Shivpuri

“Education is not just about earning a diploma; it's about shaping character, building confidence, and developing the skills that power the future.”

Welcome to Government Polytechnic College, Shivpuri, an institution dedicated to academic excellence, hands-on learning, and holistic development. It is an honor and privilege for me to serve as the Principal of this esteemed institute, where we transform young minds into skilled and responsible professionals.

At our college, we believe that technical education is the foundation of innovation and national progress. Our goal is to prepare students with not only technical knowledge but also the values and mindset needed to succeed in today’s fast-evolving world.

We offer diploma programs in:

  • Computer Science and Engineering (CSE)
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Electronics Engineering

Our college is equipped with modern laboratories, smart classrooms, and experienced faculty members who are committed to nurturing talent and igniting curiosity. We provide a balanced education that combines strong theoretical knowledge with practical, real-world applications.

To ensure student success beyond the classroom, we focus on personality development, communication skills, and industry exposure through workshops, industrial visits, and internship opportunities. Our Training & Placement Cell actively connects students with industries and supports them in launching their careers.

We believe that success comes from discipline, determination, and dedication. I encourage all students to make full use of the facilities, maintain a spirit of learning, and uphold the values of integrity and excellence.

To the parents, thank you for trusting us with your child’s future.
And to all students – dream big, stay focused, and strive to become the engineers who shape tomorrow.

With warm wishes and blessings,
— Principal
Government Polytechnic College, Shivpuri
