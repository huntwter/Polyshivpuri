FAQs


What diploma courses are offered at Government Polytechnic College, Shivpuri?

The college offers diploma programs in Computer Science & Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, and Electronics Engineering.

How can I get admission to the college?

Admissions are conducted through the Department of Technical Education, Madhya Pradesh (DTE MP) based on 10th-grade marks and online counseling. Please refer to the official DTE MP portal for updated admission procedures.

What are the hostel facilities available?

The college provides limited hostel facilities for boys. Hostel accommodations are allocated on a first-come, first-serve basis. Private hostels and PG options are also available nearby.

What is the fee structure?

The annual fee for diploma programs is approximately ₹3,000 to ₹6,000 for government seats. Additional costs may apply for hostel and exam fees. For the latest updates, please refer to the official notice.

Is there any placement assistance?

Yes, the Training & Placement Cell actively works to connect students with industries. Final year students receive training and opportunities for campus interviews, internships, and skill development programs.

Are there any scholarships available?

Yes, eligible students from reserved categories and economically weaker sections can apply for government scholarships through the MP Scholarship Portal.

Is Electronics Engineering available at the college?

Yes, Electronics Engineering is one of the core diploma branches offered at our institution, with a focus on circuits, embedded systems, and communication technologies.

Does the college have a canteen or mess?

Yes, a basic canteen facility is available on campus providing snacks, tea, and meals at affordable prices for students and staff.

What documents are required during admission?

10th Marksheet, Transfer Certificate, Domicile Certificate, Caste Certificate (if applicable), Income Certificate, Passport-size photos, and Aadhaar Card are generally required.
